Output streamflow estimates from the listed methods are provisional and subject to revision. Daily data retrieved from USGS streamgages, and NWS COOP weathers stations, is provisional data and may be inaccurate due to instrument malfunctions or physical changes at the measurement site. Subsequent review of measurements may result in significant revisions to the data and change the streamflow estimates. Streamflow data users are cautioned to consider carefully the provisional nature of the information before using it for decisions that concern personal or public safety or the conduct of business that involves substantial monetary or operational consequences. Information concerning the accuracy and appropriate uses of these data or concerning other hydrologic data may be obtained from the USGS.
